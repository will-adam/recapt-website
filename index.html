About Recapt

Recapt was built for storytellers, creators, and anyone who loves looking back on their week in a fresh way.

Our Mission

We believe your daily life deserves more than a feed of plain stats. Recapt transforms your data into creative, meaningful, and shareable stories.

Why Recapt?

With on-device rendering, your privacy comes first. You control what you share—whether it’s a festival lineup of your top songs or a receipt of your week’s highlights.

Made for Community

From seasonal templates to collaborative sharing, Recapt is designed to connect people and spark conversations around everyday moments.
